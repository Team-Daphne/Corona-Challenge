About the Developers

Hello and welcome to the coronavirus challenge! (is this redundant if we get name from storage?)

We’re on a mission to support our community by providing accurate, scientific information to help slow the spread of COVID-19. This challenge is designed to help you be informed on how to navigate different challenges during Coronavirus season.

Please fill out the user profile so we can deliver the best information for your interests and needs. Then, go through our coronavirus challenge quiz to test your knowledge of how to mitigate your risk. After each question, you’ll learn more about best practices, and after you complete the quiz you’ll see your results as well as an assortment of helpful resource links customized to your knowledge level and interest.

In this quiz, you’re aiming for the lowest score possible, so choose the answer you think is the best way to stay healthy. Your score will be tracked throughout the game, and remember that low risk choices will give you a low score! Stay safe!
